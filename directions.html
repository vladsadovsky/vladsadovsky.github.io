Parking & Building Access

Parking

Free parking is available in the building's parking lot. Look for visitor parking spaces near the main entrance.

Building Access

Enter through the main entrance and take the elevator or stairs to the second floor. Suite 218 will be clearly marked.

Accessibility

The building is wheelchair accessible with elevator access to all floors.

Public Transportation

The office is accessible via King County Metro bus routes. Check King County Metro for current schedules and routes.
